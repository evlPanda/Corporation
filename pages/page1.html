Corporation.bat is only a few months old so its relatively new compared to other corporations and alliances. The corp is hoping to get enough members to become larger and play more of a part in the Eve online economy and politics. At the moment the corp is only made up of 5 members which mine raw materials and raid small outposts and freighters. The raw materials are either sold to another player or used to create high demand ships for a profit. Each member is taxed %10 for everything they make which is then put into corporation funds which can be accessed by high ranked members of the corp.
